About the Competition

The Rubik's Cube Indian Nationals 2017 will be held on 12-14 May 2017.
The competition is open to all competitors of all nationalities. No experience in WCA competitions is necessary, but competitors should be familiar with the regulations. The competition is open to all competitors regardless of experience and skill with the Rubik's Cube, but certain event-based time limits will be in place as explained on the events tab. Media personnel should contact the organizational staff regarding media opportunities.

Location

GIIS Chinchwad, Pimpri-Chinchwad Link Road, Shridharnagar, Chinchwad, Pune, Maharashtra 411033

http://www.cubelelo.com/competitions/indian-nationals-2017-pune

Accomodation



You can book Oyo rooms for your stay at Pune for Indian Nationals 2017.

  • The occupancy in room will be as per the room size with 4 person in one room with one extra mattress.
  • The cost per person per day would be approximately Rs 500 only.
  • All rooms will have basic OYO amenities like AC, Attached Toilets, LED TV, Wi-Fi .
  • The rooms will be confirmed after 25% advance payment and the balance payment should be paid before check out.
  • The guest need to pay for anything they order for food and beverages during their stay at the time of order itself.
  • You can also contact us if you prefer personal room rather than a sharing room.
  • Please fill this form if you want accommodation – Link to Form
  • Our executive will contact you once you fill this form.
